Here you can place custom tiles by system folderto customize system tiles.

Create a folder named by system theme like snes, megadrive, mastersystem etc.. or duplicate existing folder and rename it.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
By tile custom: (Note you must choose custom pictures in theme menu for each tile).
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In each folder you can place files named:"11, 12, 13, 14, 21, 22, 23, 31, 32, 33, and 34" with .png or .jpg extension.

11 is first line and first tile, 22 is second line and second tile, 34 is third line and fourth tile etc..


In theme this will display like this:		11	12	13	14

						21	     22		23

						31	32	33	34


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
By group custom: (You must choose group tiles style in theme menu).
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can place files named "firstCorner", "secondCorner", "thirdCorner" and "lastCorner" with .png or .jpg extension.

The first corner is group of 11, 12 and 21 at upper left.

The second corner is group of 13, 14 and 23 at upper right.

The third corner is group of 21, 31 and 32 at bottom left.

The last corner is group of 23, 33 and 34 at bottom right.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
By grouping all tiles in one: (You must choose group all tiles in theme menu).
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can place a file named "allTiles" with .png or .jpg extension.

This file will be displayed in one image behind all tiles except center tile.

You can also choose in theme menu to display empty tile in center tile (22) to display custom image in all tiles (center include).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Videos.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

(Just select custom videos in theme menu for system or tile you want)
